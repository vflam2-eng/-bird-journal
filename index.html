12-week progression

Set this once. The app uses it to show Phase 1, 2, or 3.

Phase 1: Weeks 1-4

Goal: Build the habit. Keep it clean and easy.

Strength exercises use 2 sets. Carries are 2 rounds. Walks are 20-40 minutes.

Phase 2: Weeks 5-8

Goal: Add volume without adding complexity.

Most strength exercises move to 3 sets. Balance moves toward 30 seconds each side.

Phase 3: Weeks 9-12

Goal: Slightly more reps and longer carries.

Strength exercises move toward 10-12 reps. Carries move toward 45 seconds.

Back rule: any movement that irritates your low back gets lighter, smaller, slower, or skipped. This program should make life easier, not turn you into a hero with a heating pad.
